Yoodoos.com 
 Super simple HTML page hosting.
Hi.

Yoodoos is a free service to host HTML pages on your domain or sub-domain.

We just host editable HTML pages for your domain and nothing else.

How do I set it up?
Just point your CNAME to hostme.yoodoos.com or root A record to 184.72.55.6

Don't have a domain name?
Make your own sub-domain at   .yoodoos.com


How do I edit my page?
Once you've updated your DNS entries, just visit your address and append "/edit" to the URL. To keep the riff-raff out, you'll be asked to choose a secret key to save any changes.
